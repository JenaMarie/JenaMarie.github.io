hello

I'm Jena and welcome to my portfolio site.

I'm a freelance graphic designer and illustrator, hailing from Australia but currently calling London home sweet home.

I have been designing professionally for the last 6 years, in a range of smaller boutique studios.

Along with design and illustration, I have had worked across many areas of the business including: concept development, finished art, copywriting, production, client liaison, marketing and advertising.

With a love of fashion illustration, branding, contemporary art, collaboration, warm nights and cold beers, I live by the old adage,

"Love what you do and do what you love."
